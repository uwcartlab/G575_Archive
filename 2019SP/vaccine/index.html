School children are mandated by law to receive certain vaccinations. In order for herd immunity to be effective, 95% of the population needs to be vaccinated in order to protect those who cannot receive vaccinations for medical reasons.

Explore Wisconsin's public school vaccination rates by clicking on any dot on the map. Each dot represents one public school.

Use the slider on the right side of the map to filter schools by the percentage of students who have reported being up-to-date on their mandatory vaccinations.

Click anywhere on the map to start
